PROBLEM STATEMENT & BACKGROUND

  • Challenge: Higher education institutions face difficulties in efficient class scheduling
  • Root Causes:
    • Limited infrastructure and faculty constraints
    • Elective courses and overlapping departmental requirements
    • Manual preparation leads to frequent clashes
  • Impact:
    • Underutilized classrooms
    • Uneven workload distribution
    • Dissatisfied students and faculty
  • NEP 2020 Context: Increasing adoption of multidisciplinary curricula requires intelligent, adaptive solutions
Slide 3 of 10

OBJECTIVES

  • Maximize utilization of classrooms and laboratories
  • Minimize workload on faculty members and students
  • Achieve required learning outcomes efficiently
  • Provide multiple optimized timetable options
  • Enable review and approval workflow for authorities
  • Suggest suitable rearrangements when optimal solutions unavailable
  • Support multi-department and multi-shift scheduling
  • Ensure compliance with NEP 2020 guidelines
  • Reduce manual intervention and scheduling conflicts

Automated Timetable Generator 2024 Web-based solutions improve efficiency Manual intervention still needed
AI-Based Timetable System for NEP 2020 2023 AI handles multidisciplinary structures Complex constraint handling
Constraint Satisfaction in University Scheduling 1999 CSP effective for hard constraints Lacks soft constraint optimization
Summary: Existing systems show promise but lack comprehensive NEP 2020 compliance and dynamic multi-parameter optimization
